Congressman Erik Paulsen

A puppet webseries based on facts (and not the alternative ones).

Meet MN Congressman Erik Paulsen, an incumbent Republican whose district went to Clinton by 10 points, but who votes with Trump 98% of the time...and hasn't held a Town Hall since 2011. Often mistaken for bland, inanimate objects, Paulsen (along with his buddies Speaker Paul Ryan, Senator Mitch McConnell, and a chorus of old white Republicans) endeavor on zany misadventures as they navigate a hostile political landscape in the Trump era! From disastrous health care to desperate NRA endorsements to hiding the president's taxes, this rag tag team of radicals learns a little bit about the Constitution, America, and dare we say, friendship?
